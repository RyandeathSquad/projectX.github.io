Mission Statement

Our goal is to inspire underprivileged youth in the Bay Area to try new things based on their personal interests out of school. We are here to shed light on different extracurricular activities for future and current high school students who may not know what interests apply to them. To make it easier, we have formulated a short quiz to match students with one of the 5 different, broad personalities to get them started. The possibilities are endless!
Artistic An artist has patiences, persistence, and dashes of creativity to make their artwork. Whether it be the next mona lisa or another oscar nomination, artists are always passionate about their work.
Logical For people who like school. Think about it as learning the great things school couldn't teach you.
Hands-On A hands on person gets down and dirty t get the job done. They get straight to the point about getting their crafts done with elegance, persistence, and a bit of handiwork nobody can beat.
Athletic Strong, agile, and persistent. Athletes find their motivation through their own blood, sweat, and tears. Competition evokes the innermost focus of these people channeling all of their training to earn that gold medal.
Nurturer For people that care about people. Those truely kindhearted people being kind.
